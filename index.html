This project is inspired by people’s subtle facial expressions that I observed from my daily life. I name this project #maninacase, as I find that people nowadays all wear a mask on their faces. I feel like they have been making definition for themselves and decorate their “cases” in a way of what they want others to see. In one way, I would like to hold a critical view of the social phenomenon I observed and record it through illustration, in another way, I’m not fully negative to it as I treat this as a media that can help people in introspection and expression of who they are.
