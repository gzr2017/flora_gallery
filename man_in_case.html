People wear masks. I first saw them in theatres where actors, upon changing their masks, changed their personality so that the viewers would better understand what was happening on stage. To portray my style, I tied my hair in a claw braided style, with thin eyebrows and rosy cheeks.

This project is inspired by people’s subtle facial expressions that I observed and studied throughout my life. I created “masks” using multiple mediums to develop this project. My creation began with the theme of mask and redefining a person’s true self. my goal is to make my audience reexamine themselves and realize that even though sometimes wearing a mask can be necessary, they will forget their actual selves if they get addicted to it. Theories and researches based on Jung’s theory about Personality in Jungian Psychology.
